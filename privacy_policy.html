Privacy Policy for Gemini Free

Last updated: December 21, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gemini Free. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. Gemini Free is a free AI chat application powered by Google's Gemini AI.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Chat Messages: The text messages you send to the AI assistant
  • Images: Photos you choose to share with the AI for analysis (when using the image upload feature)
  • Conversation History: Your chat conversations are stored locally on your device

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • App Preferences: Your theme settings (light/dark mode) stored locally on your device
  • Advertising Data: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and advertising-related information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • AI Chat Functionality: Your messages and images are sent to Google's Gemini AI API to generate responses
  • Local Storage: Conversations are stored on your device to maintain chat history
  • App Personalization: Theme preferences are stored to provide a customized experience
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdMob

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All conversation data is stored locally on your device using Room Database. We do not store your conversations on our servers.
  • Data Transmission: Messages and images are transmitted securely to Google's Gemini AI API over encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • No Cloud Backup: Your conversation history is not backed up to any cloud service

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Your messages and images are processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate responses
  • Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Gemini AI terms: https://ai.google.dev/terms

5.2 Google AdMob

  • We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information for advertising purposes
  • AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Google Gemini AI for processing your chat requests
  • With Google AdMob for displaying advertisements
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete Conversations: You can delete individual conversations or all conversations through the app
  • Stop Using the App: You can stop using the app at any time
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Data Retention

  • Conversation data is retained on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • Data sent to Google Gemini AI is subject to Google's data retention policies

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own when using Google's Gemini AI service. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.
